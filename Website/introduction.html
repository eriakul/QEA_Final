Welcome to Taipei

Hi~ We are the Damper Babies.

Thanks for visiting our home, Taipei, the capital of Taiwan. We hope you had time to visit many of Taipei's popular attractions, such as the National Palace Museum, the Shilin Night Market, and, last but not least, the Taipei 101 skyscraper.

At a height of 1,671 feet, Taipei 101 was the world's tallest skyscraper up until 2010. Since Taipei was frequently subjected to earthquakes and typoons, Taiwan's engineers and architects had to take these external forces from nature into account during the tower's construction.

One of the precautions they took was installing a tuned mass damper at the top of the building that dampens the oscillations caused by wind and earthquakes. Essentially, it's a giant weight suspended at the top of the tower.

The Taipei 101 damper is one of the only skyscraper dampers viewable to the public and has become a significant tourist attraction.

That's where we come in. The Damper Babies are Taipei 101's very own mascots!
